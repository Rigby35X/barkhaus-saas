FAQs

Get answers to the most common questions about our pricing and platform.

Can I change plans?

Absolutely. We offer flexible plan switching with prorated billing. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time to match your shelter's changing needs.

Are there contract commitments?

No long-term contracts required. We believe in earning your trust month by month with our performance and support.

What payment methods work?

We accept all major credit cards and offer secure online payment processing. Invoice and bank transfer options are available for enterprise clients.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. We offer a 14-day free trial for all plans so you can experience Barkhaus features before making a commitment.

Do you offer discounts?

Non-profit rescues can qualify for special pricing. Contact our sales team to discuss potential discounts for your organization.
